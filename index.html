About Us

TBs gaming is a community centered around fair gameplay for its players. We come from a background where admin abuse was rampant in our online gaming communities. We felt that spoiled the fun and a bunch of us got together to create TBs gaming. We were tired of the abuse, working hard only to have things taken away by immature admins twisted by power.
